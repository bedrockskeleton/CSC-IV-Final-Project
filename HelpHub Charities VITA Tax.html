Our Purpose

How You Can Help

Your Tasks

As a VITA volunteer, you'll provide tax preparation service to taxpayers who do not have the ability to prepare their own tax returns. Some of the volunteer opportunities include being a greeter, a reviewer, a tax preparer and more. Volunteer hours are flexible. Training is free and easy. Dont know that much about taxes? Dont worry, you'll receive certification training from a certified VITA volunteer or IRS employee. After the completion of the training, you will be an IRS-certified preparer. Online training begins in November and runs through January prior to the new tax season.
